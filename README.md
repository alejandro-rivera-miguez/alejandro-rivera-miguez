# About my repositories
Welcome to my GitHub! Here you will find a curated selection of my academic, personal, and public professional projects. My work is heavily focused on combining high-fidelity CFD analysis with experimental aerodynamics, alongside robust flight dynamics and space systems engineering.

As of now, the main topics and projects covered in these repositories include:

### 💨 Computational & Experimental Aerodynamics
* **BSc Thesis - Global Flow Stability:** Eigenvalue extraction and linear perturbation analysis of laminar flows around NACA profiles (Validated with ANSYS Fluent). *(Note: Source code under NDA).*
* **Experimental Fluid Dynamics:** Wind tunnel campaigns at PoliMi featuring force balance calibration, hot-wire wake analysis, and PIV post-processing (MATLAB).
* **Automotive & Airfoil CFD:** OpenFOAM simulations of a Tesla Model S, and subsonic evaluation of the "laminar bucket" effect on NACA 6-series airfoils using SU2 and GMSH.
* **Propeller Aerodynamics:** Comparative CFD analysis of twisted vs. untwisted propeller blades (CATIA V5 & ANSYS Fluent).

### 🚀 Space Systems & Dynamics
* **Spacecraft Attitude Control:** Implementation of Extended Kalman Filters (EKF/MEKF) and LQR controllers to manage sensor noise and actuator saturation.
* **Spacecraft Systems Engineering:** Subsystem sizing for a Venus interplanetary mission, including launch trajectories, chemical/electric propulsion, power, and thermal control.
* **MoonHopper Concept:** Multidisciplinary conceptual design of a ballistic hopping exploration vehicle for the Lunar South Pole.

### ✈️ Aircraft Design & Flight Mechanics
* **AMTP Tactical Transport:** Complete conceptual and preliminary design of a military transport aircraft, with a strong focus on static and dynamic stability and control.
* **Heavy Vehicle Crosswind Stability:** Parametric study simulating rollover risks using custom Runge-Kutta solvers, Pacejka tire models, and a closed-loop PID driver model.
* **System Modeling (MSAS):** Custom ODE solvers (RK4, Newton-Raphson) and multi-domain physical modeling of a satellite's active thermal control using Simscape.

### ⚙️ Propulsion, Mechanics & PLM
* **Jet Engine Design:** Comprehensive thermodynamic modeling and off-design component matching of a Three-Spool Turboprop engine.
* **CAD/CAM & Manufacturing:** 3D assembly and 2.5-axis CNC milling simulation in CATIA V5.
* **Mechanism Dynamics:** Kinematic and Lagrangian dynamic analysis of multi-bar linkages.
* **Aerospace PLM Architecture:** Enterprise data model and lifecycle customization using Aras Innovator.

Inside each repository, you will find a dedicated README explaining the project's goals, methodology, and instructions to run the code.
